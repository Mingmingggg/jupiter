ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

For 25 years, AdCreate Society has been the top advertising organization at De La Salle University, helping students grow and unleash their creativity. While it is the home for Advertising Management students, we welcome anyone eager to explore their creative side and learn more about the world of advertising. At AdCreate, we push boundaries and build skills. Through seminars, workshops, agency tours, and our annual AdX conference, you'll gain real-world experience and industry insights. Beyond that, we focus on personal growth with team-building events, game nights, and our Neophyte Program, preparing future leaders. AdCreate isn’t just about learning—it’s about connecting! You’ll collaborate with fellow members, make lasting friendships, and develop leadership skills along the way. Our culture encourages bold ideas, shaping you into a trailblazer ready to make an impact in the competitive advertising field. Join AdCreate and be part of a community where you imagine, create, and achieve.

Mission

Equip: To equip individuals with value-added knowledge and experience of the advertising industry. Empower: To empower others by pursuing meaningful action and engagement within and outside the Lasallian community. Explore: To explore new and unfamiliar concepts that provoke thoughts and discussions that are fruitful to society and industry.

Vision

Our vision is to be an organization of students and alumni in pursuit of growth and creative innovation by imagining possibilities, creating value, and achieving progress for the betterment of and beyond the Lasallian community.
