ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The DLSU Physics Society is an organization for Physics majors and other students interested in Physics and its applications in different fields. The organization upholds the primary goal of instilling appreciation and a deeper understanding of Physics to its members and the university. The organization nurtures relations and endeavors with each other through Physics. The society was created to assist Physics students and live up to its commitment of serving alongside the Physics Department.

Mission

The DLSU Physics Society is a haven for Physics enthusiasts. Here, the members can discuss Physics topics that are interesting to them and engage in a fruitful dialogue. Physics talks are also implemented in this organization, where professors share useful and imaginative insights on having a job and career in Physics. We also conduct activities like campus tours to different Science Institutions in the Philippines.

Vision

With these activities, we hope to promote common interests and individual development of Physics majors and other students interested in the field, and we shall support the goals of the organization and advance the study and awareness of Physics in all its aspects among students and the general public.
