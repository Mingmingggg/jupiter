ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Also known as the Japan Studies Society (日本研究会), Nihon Kenkyuu Kai is the official professional organization for students currently part of the Japanese Studies Program, and a special interest organization for students in other programs. It conducts many activities and events related to Japanese studies and culture, in hopes of fostering a community of students with such similar interests, along with guiding them with many college opportunities.

Mission

The Nihon Kenkyuu Kai aims to be a professional organization capable of catering not only to the academic needs of students in the Japanese Studies Program, but also to nurture the interests of non-majors about Japan.

Vision

The Nihon Kenkyuu Kai aims to be recognized as a professional organization capable of catering to the needs of Japanese Studies Majors and cultivating special interests in the various aspects of traditional and popular Japanese Culture.
