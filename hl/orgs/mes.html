ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

MES is the official organization for all Mechanical Engineering Students in DLSU. It aims to aid the academic need of its members and, at the same time, become the official venue for the members to show and improve their talents and potential. Our purpose is to cater to the needs of the students in the Mechanical Engineering program for their overall welfare, growth, and development as future Mechanical Engineers of the country.

Mission

“To promote and enhance the total development - intellectual, spiritual, moral, social, emotional, and physical - of all its members through its quality programs and activities. MES will be the breeding ground of Lasallian individuals aspiring to be world-class, technically competent, and socially responsible mechanical engineers.”

Vision

“To become a LEADER among the various professional organizations in the university, among the different societies of mechanical engineering students in the Philippines, and among the ASME international and ASHRAE student sections in the Asia sub-region in the delivery of excellent service to its members and to the community”
