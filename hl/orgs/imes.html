ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The Industrial Management Engineering Society (IMES) is one of the leading independent professional organizations for Industrial Engineers. With 48 years of excellence and leadership, IMES is consistently ranked as one of the top organizations at De La Salle University. Driven by our goal to be one of the most influential student-led organizations, we aim to give our members the opportunities, experiences, and connections that will launch them into their futures as individuals and professionals that will shape the Philippines.

Mission

We envision Industrial Engineering students and alumni with: Professional Competence: The DLSU IE is adept at the IE discipline, with a love for learning and an ability to adjust to constant change. They are exemplars of the highest standards of professionalism. Ethics and Principle: The DLSU IE is known for its adherence to sound business practices. They live a good Christian life and are able to transmit their principles and convictions to everyone they work with. Leadership and Responsibility: The DLSU IE is a true leader, with respect for both their superiors and subordinates. They are fair and just in their use of authority, take their responsibilities seriously, and can accept and manage the consequences of their decisions.

Vision

As one of the leading professional organizations, we are committed to: Professional Development: A holistic curriculum integrated by its direct support for academic activities and its own extra-curricular activities. Ethics by example: IMES is a channel for IE students to imbibe sound Christian values and ethics in all its organizational activities through a healthy atmosphere of adherence to and respect for righteous authority. Leadership Training: IMES continuously creates opportunities for the development of the IE Community through the active involvement of all its members and officers.
