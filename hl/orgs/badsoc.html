ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The DLSU Badminton Society is a sports organization based in De La Salle University Manila. The purpose of the organization is to bring together the badminton community in DLSU for regular games and events while also promoting the sport to those who are interested.

Mission

Empowering the DLSU community through the joy of badminton, our organization is committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity, sportsmanship, and excellence. We strive to provide accessible opportunities for students, faculty, and other personnel to engage in the sport of badminton, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and personal growth. Through our dedication to skill development, education, and promoting a healthy lifestyle, we aim to make badminton a cornerstone of an active and vibrant DLSU community.

Vision

As an organization that is founded for the love of badminton, the purpose of DLSU Badminton Society lies in community building. The organization works towards providing the badminton community in DLSU the opportunity and accessibility to regularly enjoy the sport they love by organizing games and other events within the campus. The DLSU Badminton Society aims to create a strong community of badminton players and enthusiasts, uniting individuals of varying backgrounds through the love for the sport.
