ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

SEASON (Southeast Asian Studies Organization) is the home organization of the AB Southeast Asian Studies Program. It seeks to create and nurture an inclusive environment to promote mutual empowerment between its members by assuring healthy cooperations, provision of safe spaces for dialogue, and safeguarding the rights of the groupâ€™s members. Moreover, it celebrates the importance of Southeast Asian culture, people, and traditions in the modern world.

Mission

The De La Salle University Southeast Asian Studies Organization aims to promote its core values of Respect, Equality, Cooperation, Authenticity, and Service (SEARCH) through fostering community relations for proper awareness, knowledge transfer, and heightened student involvement.

Vision

The De La Salle University Southeast Asian Studies Organization envisions a community that fosters inclusivity and a peaceful society, promotes mutual empowerment, and cultivates global citizenship for all.
