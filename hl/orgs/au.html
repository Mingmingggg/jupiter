ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

ARCHERS FOR UNICEF of De La Salle University-Manila is a non-profit organization that envisions a brighter and more inclusive future for Filipino youth and women. AU continues to spearhead initiatives that are centered on advocating for the rights and well-being of children whilst achieving a holistic society supported by equitable opportunities.

Mission

To protect and advocate children's and women's rights in line with the parent organization's statement to ensure that these opportunities will be maximized for their growth and development.

Vision

AU envisions a brighter future for the Filipino youth and women through integration of Lasallian values depicted in various charitable endeavors. An organization dedicated to making a difference that will sustain its objectives and goals in hopes of achieving a holistic society that will prioritize the rights of the people.
