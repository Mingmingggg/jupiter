ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

We are the De La Salle University unit of Christ’s Youth in Action (CYA)—a movement of young people from across Asia, called to evangelize & train future leaders for the service of the Church and society. We are in over 40 colleges & youth environments across the Philippines, Singapore, & Malaysia.

Mission

We evangelize the youth and train leaders for the service of the Church and society.

Vision

We envision youth who are transformed in Christ, and are agents of positive change for the Church and society.
