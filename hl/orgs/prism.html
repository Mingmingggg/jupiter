ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

DLSU Prism is the premier LGBTQ+ organization of De La Salle University, dedicated to advancing inclusivity and equality within the community. With a passionate commitment to its mission, Prism works towards fostering a safe and welcoming environment for LGBTQ individuals and their allies. Through a diverse range of events, workshops, and initiatives, the organization seeks to raise awareness, challenge stereotypes, and advocate for the rights of the community. DLSU Prism is a beacon of hope and support, actively striving to create a more accepting and equitable future for all.

Mission

DLSU Prism, the premier LGBTQ+ organization of the De La Salle University, welcomes the proponents and allies of the marginalized and those who are subject to discrimination because of gender identity. We passionately believe in the importance of educating the Lasallian Community on the wide spectrum of gender and sexuality, including those who do not conform to the binary standards of society. Part of the organization's mantle of duties is to have an active contribution to the ongoing struggle for equal rights of the LGBTQ+ Community, whereas our collective efforts goes beyond the vicinity of the university and to a wider range of influence. We firmly believe that the welfare of each and every member of the LGBTQ+ Community is to be addressed in high priority, to which we commit ourselves to the aid of the marginalized, united by the Lasallian core values of Faith, Service, and Community.

Vision

A LGBTQIA+ organization working in excellence towards the amplification inclusivity and diversity, and advancement of equality in service of the Lasallian community and Philippine society, and coming to the defense and protection of the rights and welfare of the LGBTQIA+ community within and outside of the University.
