ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

ENGLICOM, founded in 1964, is one of the oldest and largest student organizations at De La Salle University-Manila. With over 60 years of excellence, ENGLICOM was established with a vision to promote cultural harmony and social awareness among Filipino and Chinese students. Open to everyone at the university, ENGLICOM dedicates its efforts to long-term development and nation-building by organizing social, cultural, and socio-civic events. In line with this mission, ENGLICOM serves as an avenue for its members to grow into future leaders and legends. Within ENGLICOM, members connect with others who share similar interests, build long-lasting friendships, and develop a variety of skills. Being part of ENGLICOM helps members gain exposure to different cultures, broaden their perspectives, and create opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Mission

The values encompass appreciation of both cultures, unity and integration, dedicated service and nation-building, personal growth and self-development, training and social interaction through socio-civic involvement, fostering Christian achievers for God and country, all grounded in the principles of Religio Mores et Cultura.

Vision

To continually work in awakening the Filipino-Chinese consciousness, instilling a sense of Filipino-Chinese identity, unity in spirit, compassion, and social awareness through long-term development and nation-building.
