ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants - De La Salle University Chapter (JPIA-DLSU) is the university's leading professional student organization for accounting students, spearheading and supporting initiatives for welfare and development. In line with our motto of "Bringing Balance to Life," JPIA-DLSU encourages academic, extracurricular, and socio-civic activities to strengthen each person's self-determination and accountability. The organization is also part of the National Federation of Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants (NFJPIA), an association of various Philippine JPIA chapters that aim to contribute to the development of qualified, competent, and responsible future accountants.

Mission

We aim to help our members recognize and maximize their mental, social, moral, and leadership abilities by providing opportunities for self-improvement through quality activities and services. As an organization committed to catalyzing a progressive community, we empower our members to discover and utilize their untapped potential, leading to holistic development and benefiting both themselves and the community as a whole.

Vision

Catalyzing a progressive community by transcending limits through visionary purpose.
