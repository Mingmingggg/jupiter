ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

A professional student organization that aims to promote the entrepreneurial spirit by actively providing activities that shall develop and enhance the skills, abilities, knowledge, and character of those individuals interested towards trends and innovations in business and industry; ultimately, for the entrepreneurial progress of the country.

Mission

To provide activities that develop students' skills, knowledge, and character, establish projects that encourage initiative, and act as a platform connecting students with government, businesses, and other institutions.

Vision

To promote the entrepreneurial spirit and create future leaders in business, emphasizing innovation and character development for the entrepreneurial progress of the country.
