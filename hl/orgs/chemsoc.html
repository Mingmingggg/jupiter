ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Since 1976, De La Salle University Chemistry Society had helped cultivate ideas, hone potentials, and instill camaraderie in the organization through its activities. We aim to aid the student members in fulfilling their aspirations in the field of Chemistry through personal, academic, and career development and involvement. We also aim to boost the appreciation of students for the chemical sciences and its applications.

Mission

To provide programs and services that will holistically develop our members to become service-driven, research-oriented, and responsible Lasallian chemists and citizens of the nation.

Vision

The DLSU Chemistry Society is dedicated to the formation of responsible individuals who are persons, learners, scholars, and citizens. The organization adheres to its vision of academic excellence, community development through research, and active involvement in social and environmental concerns.
