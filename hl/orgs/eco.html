ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The De La Salle University Environmental Conservation Organization (ECO) serves as the premier environmental organization for undergraduate students at both the Manila and Laguna campuses. This special-interest organization is dedicated to creating meaningful impact through a variety of initiatives aimed at addressing environmental issues. Members have the opportunity to engage in impactful activities that benefit communities and contribute to positive change. Joining ECO allows like-minded Lasallians to participate in purposeful projects and support causes that promote environmental sustainability.

Mission

Be the forerunner of spreading data-driven environmental awareness and awaken the passion for environment conservation among the members of the Lasallian Community especially the undergraduate students.

Vision

The Organization strives to center on contributing to a sustainable and healthier environment inside and outside the campus for the generation of today and tomorrow.
