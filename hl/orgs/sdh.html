ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

SDH is the premier history organization in DLSU - Manila. It is a university wide organization based in the College of Liberal Arts with members from a range of disciplines, mainly from History, Developmental Studies, and Political Science programs.

Mission

Promote an awareness and appreciation for History, Culture, and Society through Contemporary Historical Studies and History-related activities.

Vision

Sociedad de Historia is a professional student organization for history majors and history enthusiasts in DLSU - Manila which aims to be a champion for the promotion of history, culture, and society in today's ever-changing world
