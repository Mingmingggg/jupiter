ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The Economics Organization (EconOrg) is a duly recognized professional organization at De La Salle University that caters to the students in the three majors under the Carlos L. Tiu School of Economics - AB Economics, BS Industrial Economics, and BS Financial Economics. EconOrg has been the premier training ground for aspiring economists, offering a wide range of academic and extracurricular initiatives that foster both intellectual growth and holistic development among its members.

Mission

Since 1976, the Economics Organization of De La Salle University has existed to respond to the needs for the complete education and holistic development of all its constituents, while upholding the appreciation and understanding of economics in the Lasallian Community.

Vision

EconOrg is a research-based, student-led organization that aims to lay foundations on the training of future industry leaders as beacons of service and paragons of academic excellence.
