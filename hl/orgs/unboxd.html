ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

UNBOXD is a premier student-led startup organization that aims to empower the Lasallian startup space. UNBOXD does this by bridging students to its expansive network, equipping them with the skills to thrive in this industry, and training them to become startup founders and enthusiasts. UNBOXD prides itself in operating much like a startup itself, so if you're interested in diving into the world of startups or looking to becoming an industry titan, UNBOXD is your next home.

Mission

UNBOXD is committed to providing and empowering students by providing them access to the industry, equipping them with the essential skills, and creating an entrepreneurial mindset. We bridge the gap between students and the startup community, offering hands-on workshops, mentorships, and resources to inspire and guide future entrepreneurs to becoming founders and enthusiasts.
