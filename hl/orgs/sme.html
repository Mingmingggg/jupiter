ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The Society of Manufacturing Engineers DLSU Student Chapter 200, also known as SME DLSU, is the professional student organization that caters to the Manufacturing Engineering and Management (MEM) students of De La Salle University Manila. Under the banner of its mother organization, SME USA, SME DLSU is a world-class community united in empowering Lasallian engineers to become the next generation of leaders in the Philippine manufacturing industry. SME DLSU aims to uphold its mission of Industrial Consciousness, Legacy Building, and Engineering Competence through its various activities

Mission

Industrial Consciousness - To aid in the development of the Philippine manufacturing industry by improving public awareness and interest in the field through organizing informative events, strategic partnerships, and promotional campaigns. Legacy Building - To build towards a legacy of continuous improvement by empowering members with opportunities for holistic growth through the organization's events and training programs. Engineering Competence - To motivate students of the Manufacturing Engineering and Management degree program in striving for academic excellence by communicating the value of competence through career seminars and taking part in curriculum development.

Vision

A community united in helping to mold well-rounded engineers who are the forerunners in the advancement and development of the Philippine Manufacturing Industry.
