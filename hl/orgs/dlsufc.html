ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The DLSU Futsal Club is a sports organization dedicated to playing and promoting futsal and football. Here, we provide a community for like-minded people who love the sport to gather and play as a team! Everyone is encouraged to enjoy playing futsal and football, may you be a beginner or an athlete. We strive to create an inclusive environment where we can all enjoy and keep the love for the sport alive through various activities, training sessions, tournaments, and more!
