ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

The DLSU Archery Club is an inclusive and vibrant community that welcomes students, faculty, and alumni from all backgrounds, regardless of their experience level. Our club provides a supportive and welcoming environment for anyone who shares a passion for the art of archery, from beginners looking to try something new to seasoned archers seeking to improve their skills.

Mission

To empower each archer to become their best self through personalized training, support, and opportunities for growth, ensuring excellence both individually and as a team.

Vision

To create a vibrant archery community where passion, camaraderie, and shared experiences unite us, fostering lifelong connections and a love for the sport.
