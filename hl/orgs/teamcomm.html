ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Team Communication (TEAMCOMM) is the professional organization for Communication Arts and Organizational Communication students at De La Salle University. Dedicated to fostering a vibrant and progressive learning environment, TEAMCOMM empowers its members to explore and refine their skills in media production, while nurturing their creativity and interests in the arts scene. Through hands-on experiences and diverse opportunities, students are equipped to grow as responsible leaders, competent professionals, and effective communicators. As catalysts for positive change, TEAMCOMM members are prepared to impact society through their passion and talents in creation, innovation, and communication.
