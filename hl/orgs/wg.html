ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Writers' Guild is a special interest organization, providing a place writers can call home, regardless if you're a beginner or a seasoned veteran in the craft. Considering the various interests of our writers, we aim to provide safe spaces where we can flourish and support each other in our endeavors. In our shared passion for writing, we strive to redefine writing together.

Mission

Writers’ Guild shall expose its members to different areas of learning and forms of writing as an expression by means of meaningful and worthwhile activities. Furthermore, it strives to enhance its recognition to other communities by participating in external activities, creating connections with other writing organizations, and being a responsible member of society through its socio-development programs, leading to the members’ growth.

Vision

Writers’ Guild aims to uphold passion for writing among its members. It seeks to provide not only venues for inspiration and discovery but also ways for its members to broaden their writing experience. It strives to enhance its recognition of the Lasallian community as it attempts to develop the perception of the Lasallians as to the value of writing through course-related and other literary works.
