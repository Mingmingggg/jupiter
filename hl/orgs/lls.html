ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Ley La Salle is the premier professional organization for Legal Management students, aspiring lawyers, and advocates of the law of De La Salle University.

Mission

To fulfill what Ley La Salle is intended to be, emphasizing its unique brand and vision. Therefore, nurturing and producing significant contributors, not only as future exceptional individuals but also as distinguished members of Ley La Salle.

Vision

To broaden its reach beyond Legal Management students, Ley La Salle seeks to create an inclusive environment where students from all academic disciplines have the opportunity to engage in and benefit from the experiences offered by the organization.
