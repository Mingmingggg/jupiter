Tributes of the Court

In this year's Hunger Games-themed Sportsfest, teams from both CSO and non-CSO organizations will battle across three thrilling arenas: basketball, volleyball, and dodgeball. Much like tributes in the Capitol’s arena, players must rely on teamwork, strategy, and quick reflexes to survive and claim victory. The basketball tournament will test coordination and endurance, volleyball will challenge agility and precision, and dodgeball will demand sharp instincts and tactical plays. Competing in single-elimination formats, only the strongest and most unified teams will rise to the top, securing the title of champions in this year’s Tribute Sports Showdown!.

Tribute Dunk-off: Basketball Showdown

The Hunger Games-themed basketball tournament for the Annual Recruitment Week 2024: Sportsfest invites members from both CSO and non-CSO organizations to compete for glory. Divided into five clusters with two teams per cluster, a total of 10 teams will face off in a high-stakes, single-elimination format. Much like tributes in the arena, teams must use strategy, teamwork, and skill to outlast their opponents and bring honor to their organization. Only one team will rise victorious, securing the title as the champions of this year's Tribute Hoops Showdown!

Tribute Spike-off: Volleyball Showdown

The Hunger Games-themed volleyball tournament, part of the Annual Recruitment Week 2024: Sportsfest, invites both CSO and non-CSO organizations from DLSU Manila to battle for supremacy in this year’s Tribute Spike Off. Much like the tributes in the arena, teams will rely on strategy, agility, and coordination to outlast their rivals in a single-elimination format. Ten teams from various organization clusters will compete, forming alliances and using their collective strength to fight for the coveted championship title. Only one team will rise as victor, claiming glory in the spirit of the Games.

Tribute Dive-off: Dodgeball Showdown

In this Hunger Games-themed dodgeball contest, teams must eliminate all opponents by hitting them with a ball or catching their throws before the ball touches the ground, just as tributes outlast their rivals in the arena. Success depends on quick reflexes, strategy, and teamwork, with each dodge, throw, and catch mirroring the survival tactics seen in the Capitol’s games. Victory hinges on a well-balanced approach of offense and defense, much like in the Hunger Games themselves.
