Join an Organization

Join an Organization Step 1: Visit the ARW 2024 Website: cso-arw24.com Step 2: Check out the 49 CSO Organizations Step 3: Look for your desired organization’s page Step 4: Look for their respective registration forms Step 5: Answer the respective registration form and pay the membership of your chosen organization. Students are highly encouraged to pay one organization fee per transaction Step 6: Finish signing up the registration form and remember to attach the proof of payment Step 7: Visit their booths during the ARW on October 7-12, 202 Step 8: For organizations outside of CSO, you may reach out to them by visiting their booths Step 9: To learn more about the Council of Student Organizations (CSO), kindly visit their Facebook page: facebook.com/CSO.DLSU

Membership FAQs

Am I required to attend the events and games of the organizations?

Students are not required to attend the games and events of the organizations but are HIGHLY ENCOURAGED to participate to learn more about the participating organizations.

Are we required to join organizations?

No, it is not required to join organizations. However, joining an organization allows you to make your college life more memorable by meeting various people and gaining new experiences that will surely help you grow as an individual!

Is there a maximum number of organizations that I can join?

Students can join and explore as many organizations as they want.

Can I join organizations that are not related to my course?

Yes! Students are not restricted from joining various organizations as everyone can choose the organizations they want to join freely.

Am I required to join my home organization? / Is joining my home organization beneficial?

No, you are not necessarily required to join your home organization. However, joining your home organization will be beneficial as it allows you to meet students taking the same course as you do.

What are the requirements for joining an organization?

This may vary depending on the organization’s decisions.

What are the responsibilities of being a member of an organization?

The responsibilities given to members joining an organization vary depending on the organization. You may learn more about the organization’s events and what they can provide to their members by checking out our website: www.cso-arw24.com to learn more about the organizations.

Are we allowed to reserve a slot for an organization?

Strictly No! There are no slot reservations when joining an organization.

What are the perks of joining an organization?

Becoming part of an organization opens up opportunities for expanding your horizons. It encourages you to step beyond your comfort zone, interact with new individuals, acquire fresh skills, and accumulate novel experiences. These experiences offer valuable opportunities for self-discovery, helping you better understand your aspirations and strengths and ultimately benefiting your future career.

Where can I learn more about a specific organization?

You may visit the ARW 2024 Website to learn more about their details, previous events, and registration fees. Students are also encouraged to see and interact with their physical booths and freely feel the environment given by the organizations, which can be beneficial in deciding where to join.

Do we have to be a part of the Council of Student Organizations (CSO) to be a part of the central committee for every annual event under CSO? (e.g. LEAP, Frosh Welcoming, ARW, etc.)

No, there will be a central committee recruitment that an event will announce, and you can join by applying.

Do I need to have previous experience or specific skills to join an organization?

No, you do not need any previous experience or specific skills to join an organization. You are free to join any that you desire.

How do I decide which organization is the best fit for me?

You can visit the ARW booths and chat with the members of the organization regarding their activities and opportunities that you may receive by joining them!

Are there any academic benefits to joining an organization?

Some organizations, specifically home organizations, provide reviewers to their members!

Are there any leadership opportunities within organizations for freshmen?

Certainly! Organizations hold applications for Junior Officers that they will train for the whole year. Junior Officers may then opt to apply as an Associate/Assistant Vice President next academic year.
